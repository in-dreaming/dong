HTML lang Attribute Test

This test demonstrates how the lang attribute affects text shaping in different languages.

English (default): lang="en"
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
Standard English text with normal shaping.
Turkish: lang="tr"
Pijamalı hasta yağız şoföre çabucak güvendi.
i İ ı I
Turkish text with special i/İ handling. Note the dotless i (ı) and dotted I (İ).
French: lang="fr"
Portez ce vieux whisky au juge blond qui fume.
French text with accented characters and ligatures.
German: lang="de"
Zwölf Boxkämpfer jagen Viktor quer über den großen Sylter Deich.
German text with umlauts and special characters.
Arabic: lang="ar"
نص حكيم له سر قاطع وذو شأن عظيم مكتوب على ثوب أخضر ومغلف بجلد أزرق
Arabic text with right-to-left shaping and contextual forms.
Chinese: lang="zh"
今天天气很好，我们去公园散步吧。
Chinese text with CJK character shaping.
Japanese: lang="ja"
いろはにほへと ちりぬるを わかよたれそ つねならむ
Japanese text with kana and kanji shaping.
Korean: lang="ko"
다람쥐 헌 쳇바퀴에 돌아간다
Korean text with Hangul syllable shaping.
Spanish parent with English child: lang="es"
El niño juega en el parque.
The child plays in the park.
Demonstrates language inheritance: child elements inherit parent's lang attribute.
